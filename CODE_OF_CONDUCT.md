# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard
- Be direct, generous, and evidence-based.
- Critique ideas and outputs, not people.
- Prefer specific feedback over vague dismissal.
- Credit sources and state uncertainty clearly.
- Treat rigorous disagreement as healthy collaboration when it stays concrete, testable, and respectful.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, threats, discrimination, or dogpiling.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Deliberate plagiarism or fabricated citations presented as factual.
- Spam, low-effort dumping, or hostile review behavior.
- Using status, seniority, or insider language to shut down good-faith criticism without addressing the substance.
- Framing every objection as tone-policing when the actual issue is a technical, creative, or evidentiary disagreement.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions that violate this code of conduct. Repeated or severe violations may lead to a temporary or permanent ban from project spaces.

## Reporting
Report issues privately through the process in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) when safety or abuse is involved. For community conduct issues that should not live in a public thread, follow the routing in [SUPPORT.md](SUPPORT.md) and request private maintainer follow-up without posting sensitive details.

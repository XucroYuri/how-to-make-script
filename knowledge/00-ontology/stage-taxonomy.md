# Stage Taxonomy

- `ideation`: discover the raw narrative engine.
- `premise`: compress the concept into a high-clarity promise.
- `character`: define role pressure, desire, contradiction, and world fit.
- `structure`: arrange turning points and escalation.
- `outline`: sequence scenes or beats.
- `scene`: design or draft scene-level dramatic action.
- `dialogue`: tune voice, subtext, conflict, and information release.
- `rewrite`: diagnose failure modes and prioritize repair.
- `adaptation`: translate material across medium, audience, or genre constraints.

阶段 taxonomy 的意义在于把“现在该解决哪一层问题”说清楚。很多剧本返修失败，不是因为作者不努力，而是因为把结构问题当成对白问题改，把概念问题当成场景问题补。阶段一旦分清，诊断和工作流才能真正有抓手。

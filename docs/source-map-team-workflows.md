# Source Map: Team Workflow Signals

This file keeps the external basis for the repository's collaboration modes short and explicit. Some workflow implications are direct source-backed statements. Others are repository inferences.

## Episodic / Showrunner Room

- The WGA's 2023 MBA summary distinguishes development rooms and writers' rooms and ties staffing minimums to episode count and stage. Source: [WGA MBA summary](https://www.wga.org/contracts/contracts/mba/summary-of-the-2023-wga-mba).
- The WGA staff size guide gives concrete minimum staffing floors and room-duration logic. Source: [WGA staff size guide](https://www.wga.org/members/employment-resources/writers-deal-hub/staff-size-guide).
- Shondaland's public Bridgerton showrunner interview reinforces the importance of a central story owner shaping season-level decisions. Source: [Shondaland interview](https://www.shondaland.com/shondaland-series/shondaland-bridgerton-behind-the-scenes/a60702896/bridgerton-showrunner-jess-brownell/).
- Inference: episodic team modes need a strong convergence owner plus room-friendly artifact stages.

## Animation / Story Trust

- Disney's Disneytoon interview explicitly names the Story Trust as a peer-review process and describes research and constructive criticism as central to story development. Source: [Disneytoon interview](https://thewaltdisneycompany.com/news/disneytoon-executive-on-the-animation-studio-behind-the-pirate-fairy-and-planes-fire-rescue/).
- Inference: animation-oriented collaboration modes should give peer review and world research their own workflow lanes instead of treating them as optional polish.

## Branded / Campaign Teams

- Wieden+Kennedy's Three "Pony" case shows one central brand expression expanded across TV, digital, and a participatory share mechanic. Source: [W+K case](https://www.wk.com/work/three-pony/).
- Google's Taxfix case says traditional video production was too slow for iterative testing, and documents an AI-first, human-steered, multi-variant workflow with rapid turnaround and explicit strategic control. Source: [Google / Taxfix case](https://business.google.com/en-all/think/search-and-video/taxfix-veo-youtube-ai-first/).
- Inference: branded team modes need a message spine, fast variant lanes, and a strong human brand-fit gate.

## Interactive / Narrative Systems

- Ubisoft's narrative design article says narrative designers collaborate with game design, audio, production, and quest-design teams, and describes narrative design as a cross-disciplinary role between mechanics and narrative. Source: [Ubisoft narrative design article](https://news.ubisoft.com/en-gb/article/7m412GLSbfkaT0YheRYLVG/what-is-narrative-design).
- Ubisoft's Story Creator Mode article describes browser-based story creation, a content hub, and in-game execution as one integrated flow. Source: [Story Creator Mode article](https://news.ubisoft.com/en-us/article/1yMR91cFZCtrN7McYV4oeD/how-assassins-creed-odysseys-story-creator-mode-actually-works).
- Inference: interactive narrative modes should treat systems, state, and implementation as first-class collaboration lanes.

## Agent-Orchestration Patterns

- OpenAI's Agents SDK documentation distinguishes manager-style orchestration (`Agent.as_tool()`), handoffs, structured code orchestration, evaluator loops, and parallel runs for independent subtasks. Source: [OpenAI Agents SDK](https://openai.github.io/openai-agents-python/multi_agent/).
- The oh-my-openagent README foregrounds discipline agents, parallel background specialists, scoped tool/context loading, and category-based model routing. Source: [oh-my-openagent README](https://github.com/code-yeongyu/oh-my-openagent).
- Inference: the repository should model screenplay collaboration as selectable operating modes plus explicit handoff contracts and bounded context packs, not as one monolithic "multi-agent" behavior.

## Note

- Where a source states a staffing fact, workflow structure, or collaboration pattern directly, this map treats it as source-backed.
- Where the repository turns that source into a reusable mode, artifact ladder, or gating rule, this map treats it as inference.

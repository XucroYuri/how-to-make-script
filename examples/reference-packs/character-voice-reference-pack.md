# Character Voice Reference Pack

This pack uses original synthetic fragments to teach character-voice calibration.
They are reference paths, not mandatory templates.

## 1. Suppressed Anger In A Family Drama

### Stronger Sample

```text
女儿把水杯放回桌上，放得太稳了。

女儿：你别每次都等事情过去了，才来问我疼不疼。
父亲：我是在问你现在。
女儿：现在？现在你问得起，是因为那时候你躲得开。
```

### Weaker Contrast

```text
女儿：其实我一直很生气，因为你小时候没有保护我。你现在来关心我已经太晚了。
父亲：我知道你受伤很深。
```

### Why The Stronger Version Works

- The daughter's anger appears through control, not summary.
- The lines carry accusation, memory, and current power struggle at once.
- Her voice feels lived-in because the scene pressure changes the rhythm and restraint.

### Why The Weaker Version Fails

- It explains the emotion instead of dramatizing it.
- The daughter sounds like an analyst of herself, not a person in conflict.
- The father becomes a functional reply node instead of a pressured participant.

### Non-Dogma Note

Some scenes should be more direct. The point is not "always suppress emotion." The point is to match the expression to the relationship pressure.

## 2. IP Continuity Without Imitation

### Stronger Sample

```text
队友：你今天怎么这么安静？
主角：我不是安静，我是在给你的烂计划留遗言时间。
队友：那你还跟来？
主角：因为你要是真死了，我还得替你解释。
```

### Weaker Contrast

```text
队友：你今天怎么这么安静？
主角：哼，我还是老样子，只不过命运总爱捉弄我。你知道我的风格。
```

### Why The Stronger Version Works

- The voice is preserved through stance, sarcasm, and reluctant loyalty.
- It sounds like the same person in a new scene, not like a quotation machine.
- The rhythm and value ordering stay continuous without copying prior iconic lines.

### Why The Weaker Version Fails

- It tries to signal continuity by announcing continuity.
- The character starts describing their brand instead of embodying it.
- Familiarity is reduced to self-reference and wink-at-the-audience writing.

### Non-Dogma Note

Some IPs do have repeatable signature phrases. They should be accents, not the whole continuity model.

## 3. Premium Brand Voice Without Fake-Premium Drift

### Stronger Sample

```text
镜头里，女人把晚归时皱掉的衬衫挂平，洗手台灯刚好亮起。

旁白：不是每一天都从容。
旁白：但你还是希望，照镜子的时候，先看见自己回来了。
```

### Weaker Contrast

```text
旁白：以非凡精粹，唤醒奢雅新生。
旁白：每一次触碰，都是高定人生的璀璨绽放。
```

### Why The Stronger Version Works

- It earns aspiration through a lived moment before abstract promise.
- The tone stays elevated without severing contact with ordinary life.
- The product space feels human first, persuasive second.

### Why The Weaker Version Fails

- The language is generic luxury filler with no lived anchor.
- Nothing in the wording signals a real user, context, or emotional occasion.
- It sounds expensive, but not believable.

### Non-Dogma Note

Some luxury campaigns intentionally embrace stylization. Even then, abstraction works better when it grows out of a clear sensory or social situation.

## 4. Interactive Suspect Who Deflects Like A Person

### Stronger Sample

```text
玩家：案发那晚你到底去哪了？
嫌疑人：你们问这个，像是已经知道答案了。
玩家：你可以先回答。
嫌疑人：我可以。只是我得先知道，你们打算拿谁的话当真。
```

### Weaker Contrast

```text
玩家：案发那晚你到底去哪了？
嫌疑人：我现在不想告诉你，因为我是一个可疑角色。请选择下一个问题。
```

### Why The Stronger Version Works

- The suspect resists, tests power, and protects themselves in real time.
- The dialogue keeps the interrogation playable while preserving character strategy.
- Information delay comes from self-protection, not from a game-state placeholder.

### Why The Weaker Version Fails

- The line exposes system intent instead of character intent.
- The suspect has no self-preserving worldview, only a blocking function.
- The scene stops feeling like an interrogation and starts feeling like UI.

### Non-Dogma Note

Interactive dialogue still needs clarity for the player. The goal is not maximal opacity. The goal is to let clarity and character pressure coexist.
